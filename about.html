WHY I DO WHAT I DO

I Believe Software Should Be As Reliable As It Is Responsive. My Background Involves Two Distinct Disciplines: The Strict, Structured World Of Java Object-Oriented Design, Where I Focus On Architecture And Data Integrity, And The Fast-Paced World Of Web Development, Where I Use AJAX And PHP To Create Seamless, Non-Blocking User Experiences. I Aim To Build Applications That are solid on the backend and snappy on the frontend. I also am fascinated with how Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Has Changed The Industry And That Is A Field I Also Aspire To Be Involved In Sometime In The Near Future. The Tech Industry Requires Constant Evolution Due To How Rapidly It Changes And That Is The Aspect Of Tech That Fascinates Me The Most.
